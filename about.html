WHO IS Heavens?

At Heavens, we specialise in student accommodation tailored to university life. Our team focuses on affordable, well-located rooms and apartments close to major campuses and public transport.

We make moving simple: flexible lease lengths to match semesters, furnished options for immediate move-in, and utility-inclusive packages to keep costs predictable. Every property is vetted for safety and managed by local agents who understand student needs.

Whether you're a first-year looking for a supportive houseshare or a postgraduate seeking a quiet self-contained flat, Heavens helps you find a home that fits your budget and lifestyle.

Need help finding the right place? Use our student listings to filter by distance to campus, price, and amenities, or contact our student housing team for personalised assistance.

We're committed to providing responsive support—from viewing bookings to move-in coordination and ongoing property management—so you can focus on your studies.
